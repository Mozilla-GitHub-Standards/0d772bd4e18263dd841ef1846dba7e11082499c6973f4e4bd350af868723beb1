Mozilla Application Services

Mozilla Application Services helps you manage your online life by giving you a central location to store your online identity, including the bringing together of all your address books and friend lists, the services you use to share links etc.

Once you have set up Mozilla Application Services, web applications that use the Open Web Applications can use these facilities no additional setup required. (You will always be in control of access to your data, of course!)

Mozilla will never sell your address lists to anyone or use them to contact your friends without your permission.

To get started, click on one of these services to login via OpenID
